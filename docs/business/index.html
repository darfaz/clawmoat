An AI agent is a persistent AI assistant that runs on a dedicated machine and can execute real tasks ‚Äî managing email, scheduling, research, data analysis, coding, customer support drafts, and more. Unlike ChatGPT, it runs 24/7, remembers context across sessions, and can take autonomous actions. Businesses use them to multiply team productivity without hiring.

You can ‚Äî it's open source. But out-of-the-box, AI agents have unrestricted access to your filesystem, credentials, and network.
